BopperX

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2025

BopperX is a web-based content management tool designed for creators and agencies to manage and publish TikTok videos. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles data.

1. Data We Collect

BopperX does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third parties. The application operates entirely on your local device.

  • TikTok API tokens — OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored locally in a tokens.json file on your device. They are never sent to any server other than TikTok's official API endpoints.
  • Video files — Videos you place in the upload queue are only transmitted directly to TikTok's upload servers when a post is scheduled or triggered.
  • Log files — Activity logs are stored locally on your device and are never shared externally.

2. How We Use TikTok Data

The application uses the video.publish scope to post videos to your TikTok account on your behalf. No TikTok user data (followers, analytics, profile information) is accessed or stored.

3. Third-Party Services

BopperX communicates exclusively with TikTok's official API (open.tiktokapis.com). No data is shared with any other third party.

4. Data Retention

All data (tokens, logs, video files) is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by removing the application files.

5. Children's Privacy

BopperX is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
